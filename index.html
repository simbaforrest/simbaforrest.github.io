I graduated in 2015, and I'm currently visiting Mitsubishi Electric Research Laboratories (MERL) as a visiting researcher.

My advisor is Professor Vineet R. Kamat. I previously graduated from Wuhan University in China with BEng in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing.

My research interests include Computer Vision, Photogrammetry, Robotics, Machine Learning, Augmented Reality, and their applications in civil engineering.
